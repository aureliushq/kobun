### Introduction

The exploration of space has been a cornerstone of human achievement, driven by curiosity and the pursuit of knowledge.

### Early Attempts

- **19th Century**: Tethered balloons were used for early atmospheric experiments.
- **20th Century**: The invention of rockets marked the beginning of modern space travel.

### Key Milestones

1. **1969**: Apollo 11 moon landing - first human on the Moon.
2. **1983**: First Space Shuttle launch - Challenger.
3. **2001**: ISS operations begin - continuous human presence in space.

### Challenges

- Extreme conditions, radiation, and gravity present significant challenges for astronauts and machines.

### Future Directions

Space exploration continues to evolve with new technologies like reusable rockets and deeper space missions.
